AUTOSAR is a standardized automotive software architecture.
Classic AUTOSAR is designed for microcontrollers and resource-constrained ECUs.
Adaptive AUTOSAR targets high-performance computing platforms.

ADAS perception systems rely on sensors such as cameras, LiDAR, radar, and ultrasonic sensors.
Sensor fusion combines multiple sensor inputs to improve accuracy.

OTA (Over-the-Air) allows remote software updates for ECUs.
Diagnostics are used to detect and troubleshoot faults in vehicle systems.
